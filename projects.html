Coin Apex is a NYC-based tech incubator that was born in fall of 2013 to give talented people a place to create and build cool things. While at Coin Apex, I handled operations, investor relations, client and banking services.

Coin.co is a private company that facilitates payment processing for the virtual currency Bitcoin.Through the use of proprietary software, Coin.co allows merchants to reap the beneﬁts of the emerging digital currency market without having to deal with the actual accounting or processing of virtual currency. Coin.co merchants accept Bitcoin, yet receive USD.

Snatch is a mobile application that allows the user to "match" with snacks in their area based on their real-time GPS location and snack preferences. Snatch uses a playful, interactive and simple interface that lets you make quick, shareable snack-ordering decisions on the go.
